# Lead Inbox

**Lead Inbox** is a B2B SaaS platform that solves the lead generation problem for business consulting agencies. Instead of spending hours manually searching for prospects, consulting firms subscribe and receive verified, niche-specific leads delivered directly to their inbox every week.

---

## The Problem

Consulting agencies — especially boutique and mid-sized firms — struggle to maintain a consistent pipeline of qualified leads. Outbound prospecting is time-consuming, inconsistent, and pulls attention away from billable work.

## The Solution

Lead Inbox sources leads from third-party data providers, filters them by consulting category, and delivers them directly to the subscriber's email on a recurring basis. No dashboard to log into, no manual searching — just relevant leads, ready to act on.

---

## Features

- **Category-based filtering** — Firms receive only leads relevant to their specific area of consulting, eliminating noise and irrelevant contacts.
- **Weekly email delivery** — Verified, outbound-ready leads are delivered directly to the subscriber's inbox on a recurring schedule.
- **Subscription-based access** — Firms subscribe monthly and receive a consistent, ongoing pipeline of leads without any manual effort.
- **Third-party verified data** — Leads are sourced from trusted data providers, ensuring quality and accuracy.

---

## How It Works

1. A consulting firm visits the platform and selects their consulting category.
2. They subscribe via a monthly payment.
3. Every week, verified leads matching their category are delivered straight to their inbox — no login required.
